Our Mission

The Bridge Project is dedicated to preventing hate crimes and fostering inclusive communities across the Bay Area. Founded in 2018, we work with law enforcement, community leaders, and citizens to create safer spaces for everyone.

Our approach combines education, community engagement, and rapid response to incidents. We believe that understanding and empathy are the strongest tools against hate.

Since our inception, we've trained over 15,000 community members, responded to 300+ incidents, and helped reduce hate crimes in targeted neighborhoods by 42%.
